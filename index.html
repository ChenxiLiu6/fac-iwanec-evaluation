FAC Speech Evaluation

Foreign Accent Conversion — Subjective Listening Test

This evaluation consists of 3 short listening tests:

  • Test 1: AB Accentedness Preference (30 items) Listen to two clips of the same sentence. Choose which sounds more native-like.
  • Test 2: XAB Speaker Similarity (12 items) Listen to a reference speaker (X), then two clips (A, B). Choose which sounds more like X.
  • Test 3: 5-Point Naturalness MOS (36 items) Listen to a clip and rate how natural it sounds on a 1-5 scale.

Estimated time: 10 ∼ 15 minutes. Please use headphones. You can pause and resume anytime.
